Sports & Remedial Massage Therapy

A targeted, evidence-based manual therapy session designed to identify and resolve musculoskeletal (MSK) issues and correct faulty movement patterns.

Your session begins with a short consultation and diagnostic assessment to identify the root cause of your discomfort. The treatment is a hands-on intervention using advanced techniques (Myofascial Release, Joint Mobilisation, Muscle Energy Technique) to restore tissue health, resolve pain, and optimise your movement.

  • Targeted relief for acute and chronic muscle pain.
  • Focused on restoring tissue health and movement range.
  • Essential component of injury prevention and performance.
